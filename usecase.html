Six Questions to Find AI Opportunities
▼
1
Find the Business Value

"Will this idea make money, save money, or save face (fewer errors, better compliance, reduced risk)?"

2
Find the Hidden Insights

"What data do you collect but never really analyse? Where would a prediction, trend, or early warning change how you make decisions?"

3
Find the Pain

"What process causes the most frustration, errors, or overtime in your team?"

4
Find the Repetition

"What do you spend most of your time on that follows roughly the same steps every time?"

5
Find the Bottlenecks

"Where do things get stuck, bounce between people, or sit waiting for someone to act?"

6
Find the Rekeying

"Where do you copy information from one system or document into another?"
